Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. Who We Are

MessageAI is a personal SMS-based AI assistant that helps users find restaurants and place food delivery orders via text message.

2. Information We Collect

We collect your phone number when you text our service. We also store your delivery address (encrypted) and order history to personalize your experience. We do not collect payment card numbers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to respond to your messages, fulfill food orders, and improve your experience. We do not sell, share, or distribute your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using AES-256 encryption. Sensitive data such as delivery addresses are encrypted at rest.

5. SMS Messaging

By texting MessageAI, you consent to receive SMS responses related to your requests. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as you use the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, reply HELP to any MessageAI message or text (801) 346-2263.
